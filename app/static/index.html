      Welcome to the studio

      Four moves take you from a GPX file to a printed poster and a feed post:

      1. ◳
        Compose — drop your tracks; your map appears, then frame the poster. No plate for your area yet builds one from USGS terrain.
      2. ✦
        Style · Layers · Light — render a proof, then tune it live. Every edit re-proofs itself.
      3. ◉
        Social — repurpose it for Reels, feed posts and stories, with safe zones and a caption.
      4. ⇩
        Exports — accept to render the full-resolution final; every render lands here.

      Tip: press ? any time for shortcuts, or ⌘K to search every option (it can reopen this guide too).
